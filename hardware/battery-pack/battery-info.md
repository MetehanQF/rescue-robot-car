# Rescue Robot Battery Pack

Battery Type: 18650 Li-ion

Configuration: 3S2P

Cells used: Samsung 25R

Battery pack assembled manually using spot welding / soldering.

Tested voltage after assembly: 12.4V

Nominal Voltage: 11.1V  
Max Voltage: 12.6V

Capacity: 2600mAh × 2 = 5200mAh

BMS: 3S 40A BMS
Protection:
- Overcharge protection
- Overdischarge protection
- Short circuit protection

Connector: XT60

Usage: Powering motor drivers and DC-DC converters for robot electronics.

Components:
- 6x Samsung 25R 18650 cells
- 3S 40A BMS
- XT60 connector
- Nickel strips
- Fish paper insulation
- Heat shrink
